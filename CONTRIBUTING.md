# Enter The Wu-Tang (36 Chambers of Contributing)

> "Sharp like a blade, precise in our ways" - Ghostface Killah

We welcome all warriors to contribute to this sacred knowledge. Here's how to join the clan:

## Adding New Techniques

1. Fork the scrolls (repository)
2. Create your chamber (branch)
3. Perfect your style (changes)
4. Show your skills (pull request)

## The Templates

These templates are battle-tested. When updating:

1. Test like a grandmaster
2. Keep it responsive like flowing water
3. Make it accessible to all warriors
4. Document your techniques

## Testing Your Style

Before sharing your knowledge:
1. Product display must be sharp
2. Cart must flow like water
3. Checkout must be secure
4. Mobile views must be fluid
5. All browsers must respect it

## Spreading Knowledge

When adding new techniques:
1. Update the sacred texts (SKILL.md)
2. Show examples of your style
3. Share wisdom for troubleshooting

## Need Guidance?

Open an issue for:
- Bugs (weaknesses in the style)
- Features (new techniques)
- Documentation (clarity of teaching)

## The Code of Conduct

1. Respect all contributors
2. Test everything thoroughly
3. Share knowledge freely
4. Keep it Wu-Tang forever

Remember: Wu-Tang is for the children. Make your contributions educational and accessible.
